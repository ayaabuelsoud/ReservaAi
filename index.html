How it Work

1

Start chatting

visit the page and click on send Message and start talking with the bot "hello" will make the bot tell you example phrases that it can understand.

2

Ask for available products/services

for example "What are the available movies ?" , "What are the available comedy movies ?" the bot will list all the products or products based on a category if it exists.

3

Ask for reservation

For example "I want to buy 2 tickets for avengers end game movie next friday" or "I want to reserve 5 tickets for spiderman movie tomorrow" if the bot finds the item exists it'll place a reservation.

4

Ask for past reservations

for example "please show my reservations" or "show my orders" it'll show your orders while it's being processed by the admins
